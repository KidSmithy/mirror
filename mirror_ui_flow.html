05 — How it works

Two agents. One self.

Built on Vertex AI Managed Agents (I/O 2026). The Therapist and the Mirror share memory through Firestore + Vertex AI vector search, but speak with different voices, on different cadences.

Conscious Layer
The Therapist
Warm, Socratic, attachment-informed. Real-time chat and journal companion. Never confronts. Asks the next gentle question.
Gemini · Real-time
Unconscious Layer
The Mirror
Runs across your full journal + chat history. Detects defenses, omissions, repetitions. Speaks once a week with evidence the user wrote themselves.
Vertex Agent · Weekly

Gemini 2.x

Multi-turn empathetic dialogue for the Therapist. Multimodal voice + image input.

Vertex AI Managed Agents

Orchestrates Therapist ↔ Mirror. Shared memory. Independent personas.

Imagen / Gemini multimodal

Generates the monthly attachment landscape from your data signals.

Confidential Computing

Sensitive journal content processed in enclaves. Privacy as a brand pillar.
